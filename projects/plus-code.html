Technical story

PLUS-CODE+ is a zero-installment rover indoor localization system that combines OCR-based visual RTK, GNSS-dead reckoning, and DNN-based adaptive fusion to support indoor navigation without pre-installed localization infrastructure.

Paper: A. Khudoyberdiev, H. Young Kim, and J. Ryoo, “PLUS-CODE+: Zero-Installment Rover Indoor Localization,” IEEE Sensors Journal, 2025.

Result highlights to show

  • Show indoor–outdoor transition performance.
  • Show vRTK correction effect over GNSS-dead-reckoning drift.
  • Show localization accuracy plots and multi-floor map matching.
