Technical story

MirrorVision is a lightweight robot-vision system for elevator floor detection under occlusion, using direct and reflective views of elevator number panels from pre-installed mirrors.

Paper: A. Khudoyberdiev and J. Ryoo, “MirrorVision: Light-Weight Floor Detection System for an Autonomous Robot in a Crowded Elevator,” Journal of Image and Graphics, 2024.
