Technical story

CLOUD-CODEC targets large-scale traffic-camera storage by encoding structured traffic scenes with lightweight DNN object detection and box-shaped segmentation rather than storing every pixel in a conventional video stream.

Paper: H. Kim, A. Khudoyberdiev, S. S. R. Garnaik, A. Bhattacharya, and J. Ryoo, “CLOUD-CODEC: A New Way of Storing Traffic Camera Footage at Scale,” ACM TOMM, 2025.
