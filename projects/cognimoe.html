Technical story

CogniMoE performs end-to-end multimodal mental workload classification using on-the-fly scalogram generation, per-modality temporal encoders, and Mixture-of-Experts gating for adaptive fusion.

Paper: K. P. Ismaylovna, A. Khudoyberdiev, and H.-C. Kim, “CogniMoE: End-to-End Multimodal Mental Workload Classification via On-the-Fly Scalogram Generation and MoE Gating,” IEEE Sensors Journal.
